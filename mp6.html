Welcome to Kelly Guthrie's MP6

For my mini project 6, I decided to make a video about Khan Academy. The video showcases the different features and tools of the website. The video shows the different ways you can use the website with students and why using Khan Academy is very beneficial for students, especially in math. I made the video with Imovie but converted it to a wmv file. I used 5 different transitions between slides. I included a title slide and a credit slide to Khan Academy at the end. The music is very positive and upbeat. I had to create slides with words to introduce different aspects of Khan Academy.
